The Great Commission entails making disciples, which accounts for half of its importance, and this is best achieved through communal effort. By participating in Harvest Groups, you'll not only find a sense of belonging and camaraderie but also develop a deeper understanding of God's teachings and your life's mission. We welcome individuals from all walks of life to join us and become part of our community.

While we value the experience of congregational worship, there's an undeniable charm to gathering in smaller, more intimate settings such as living rooms, classrooms, or dining tables. These settings inspire us to approach worship with a fresh perspective, different from what we experience in larger spaces. By fostering deeper connections with each other in these intimate settings, we can all thrive and grow together.

The groups will convene either once a week or every other week, typically for a duration of 1-2 hours. You have the freedom to select a group that aligns with your preferred meeting frequency at the time of registration. Our groups usually take breaks during holidays and may have a reduced meeting schedule during the some months. However, the decision to meet during these periods lies with your group leader and the group's collective preference.

Harvest Groups offer a space where individuals can foster genuine connections and nurture their spiritual development. As a member of a group, you'll engage in social activities, engage in learning, and participate in prayer sessions. You need only attend with an open mind and genuine intentions to connect and grow.
