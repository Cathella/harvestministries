About Us

Harvest Teaching Ministries is a dynamic and flourishing church that was established in 1995 by Pastor Gerald Mukuye and his wife. The church's primary objective is to guide individuals into a genuine and transformative relationship with Jesus Christ. The church promotes a life-giving and optimistic culture where people can worship God, connect with one another, and discover their divine purpose.

Our goal is to guide individuals towards a genuine and transformative connection with Jesus Christ that can change their lives.

Our aspiration is to make a significant impact on the world. We do not view this as an insurmountable task since we serve a God who utilizes ordinary individuals to accomplish remarkable feats. Our objective is to transform lives through numerous physical locations worldwide, influence millions of people via our online community. We invite you to join us and assist us in fulfilling God's vision!
