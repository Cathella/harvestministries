Latest Features

Sunday's Sermon

Last Sunday's sermon stressed regulating churches due to a tragic incident in Kakira, where a pastor took a child's life following misguided advice from a senior pastor. This highlighted the importance of oversight. The message emphasized cultivating forgiving hearts for genuine repentance, noting its humble nature. Neglecting forgiveness for true change was cautioned against, as many battle hardened hearts that dismiss sincere repentance. The sermon concluded by emphasizing the need for compassionate hearts to extend forgiveness.

Water Baptisms

Several individuals were baptized in water. The pastor explained the significance of the baptism, emphasizing the believers' new life in Christ. Each person shared their testimony of accepting Jesus as their Savior, and the congregation cheered as they were immersed in water. The service ended with a time of prayer for the newly baptized and for those who wanted to receive Jesus as their Lord and Savior.

About Us

Join us on a journey of spiritual growth and discovery. Through our teachings, resources, and vibrant community, we invite you to dive into the richness of God's Word, embracing His promises as a pathway to abundant life. Our desire is for you to experience the transformative power of His love and grace, leading to a deeper understanding of His truth and a life that is aligned with His divine nature. In a world filled with distractions and challenges, we strive to provide a sanctuary where you can find solace, guidance.
